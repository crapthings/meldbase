# Meldbase collaboration notes

## Local playground boundary

`local-playground/` is the maintainer's private, ignored demonstration and
testing workspace. Do not add, move, publish, or describe its contents as a
released Meldbase example unless the maintainer explicitly removes that
boundary. Its generated data, credentials, dependencies, and UI are outside
the repository's product surface.

## Current focus

Prioritize the Meldbase database itself: its core storage, server contract,
SDKs, operational tooling, verification, and published documentation. Treat
the local playground only as an optional manual test environment.
